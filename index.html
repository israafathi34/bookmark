Rules for writing a valid url

  1. Start with the protocol: Include "http://" or "https://" at the beginning.
  2. Use a descriptive domain: Choose a clear and meaningful domain name.
  3. Add specific path: Include a concise path to the desired resource.
  4. Separate directories with slashes: Use "/" to separate directories in the path.
  5. Encode special characters: Replace spaces and non-alphanumeric characters.
  6. Mind lowercase or uppercase: Be consistent with letter case in URLs.
  7. Keep it simple: Avoid unnecessary complexity in URLs.
  8. Be clear and meaningful: Create URLs that accurately represent the content.
